
CITATION

The passport data and the trait experiment data are available online from the USDA NPGS GRIN database (http://www.ars-grin.gov/cgi-bin/npgs/html/desc.pl?65049, verified 17 Dec 2010). Bonman et al. (2007) described the experimental design for the field trials. The ICARDA ecoclimatic layers were extracted from the ICARDA ecoclimatic information system (De Pauw, 2008). The WorldClim ecoclimatic layers were extracted from the online WorldClim dataset (http://www.worldclim.org; Hijmans et al., 2005). The combined data set based on these three sources that is available here was developed by Endresen et al. (2011).


Bonman, J.M., H.E. Bockelman, Y. Jin, R.J. Hijmans, and A.I.N. Gironella. 2007. Geographic Distribution of Stem Rust Resistance in Wheat Landraces. Crop Science 47:1955-1963. DOI: 10.2135/cropsci2007.01.0028

Endresen, D. T. F., Street, K., Mackay, M., Bari, A., and de Pauw, E. (2011). Predictive association between biotic stress traits and ecogeographicaldata for wheat and barley landraces. Crop Science 51: 2036-2055. DOI:10.2135/cropsci2010.12.0717

Hijmans, R.J., S.E. Cameron, J.L. Parra, P.G. Jones, and A. Jarvis. 2005. Very high resolution interpolated climate surfaces for global land areas. International Journal of Climatology 25:1965-1978. DOI: 10.1002/joc.1276.
